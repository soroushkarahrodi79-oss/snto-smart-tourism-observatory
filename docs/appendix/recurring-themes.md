# Recurring Themes

## Purpose
Capture recommendations repeated across multiple independent reviews.

## Background
The strongest conclusions are those repeated by scientific, operational, product, and engineering reviewers.

## Current State
Highest-confidence recurring themes:
1. SNTO should be positioned as protected-area decision intelligence, not a generic GIS or dashboard.
2. The core workflow must be asset risk -> confidence -> action -> budget.
3. Field validation is the main scientific blocker.
4. Enterprise security, auth, audit, and tenancy are hard blockers for institutional customers.
5. The current experience shows too much methodology in the operational surface.
6. SNTO should integrate with GIS and Earth observation platforms rather than replace them.
7. Real, calibrated, synthetic, and simulated evidence must remain strictly separated.
8. The strongest innovation is the evidence-to-decision chain.

## Evidence
These themes recur in the protected-area director, scientific board, CTO, CPO, and international consensus reviews.

## Recommendations
Treat these as strategic constraints until superseded by future evidence.

## Next Steps
Use these themes to prioritize v1.1, v1.2, and v2.0 planning.
