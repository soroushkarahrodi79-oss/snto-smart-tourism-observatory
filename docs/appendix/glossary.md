# Glossary

## Purpose
Define strategic terms used across SNTO documentation.

## Background
The reviews repeatedly used product, scientific, and enterprise terms that need stable meanings.

## Current State
- Decision intelligence: the translation of evidence into defensible management decisions.
- Protected natural tourism destination: a protected area or natural destination where conservation and visitor use must be managed together.
- EHS: Environmental Health Score.
- DCS: Decision Confidence Score.
- SCM: Spatial Causality Module or spatial impact-gradient reasoning.
- TPI: Territorial Priority Index.
- TIS: Tourism Intervention Score or intervention prioritization score.
- Enterprise readiness: ability to support secure, governed, auditable, multi-user institutional use.
- Operational workflow: recurring real-world task flow used by managers, analysts, rangers, or reporting officers.

## Evidence
These terms were used across the repository audit, scientific review, CTO review, CPO review, and consensus review.

## Recommendations
Use these definitions consistently in future documentation and product copy.

## Next Steps
Add new terms only when they are used by multiple strategic documents.
