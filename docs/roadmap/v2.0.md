# v2.0 Roadmap

## Purpose
Define v2.0 as the modular product architecture and UI-evolution release.

## Background
v2.0 is the release where SNTO stops being primarily a dashboard and becomes a modular operational product.

## Current State
SNTO lacks platform foundations for enterprise use and should not expose major operational workflows until persistence, identity, audit, and role separation are designed.

## Evidence
The CTO review identified auth, audit, tenancy, persistence, API discipline, observability, and background jobs as blockers.

## Recommendations
v2.0 priorities:
- modular product architecture;
- role-based workflows;
- persistent product backend;
- identity and authorization;
- audit trail;
- intervention lifecycle;
- field verification workflow;
- versioned API;
- observability;
- major UI evolution into director, analyst, field, reporting, and audit surfaces.

## Next Steps
Treat v2.0 as the earliest candidate for serious institutional adoption, but not yet as global category leadership.
