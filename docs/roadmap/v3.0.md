# v3.0 Roadmap

## Purpose
Define v3.0 as the enterprise and institutional platform-maturity release.

## Background
v3.0 should scale validated workflows across multiple protected areas and agencies after v2.0 platform foundations exist.

## Current State
International generalizability is unproven.

## Evidence
The consensus review required multi-park deployment, enterprise security, partner ecosystem, benchmarking, public portal, and measured outcomes.

## Recommendations
v3.0 priorities:
- multi-park and network deployment;
- benchmarking;
- national or agency-level rollups;
- enterprise deployment governance;
- partner ecosystem;
- public transparency portal where appropriate;
- measured conservation and management outcomes;
- international validation and case studies.

## Next Steps
Use v3.0 to move from institutional adoption to category leadership.
