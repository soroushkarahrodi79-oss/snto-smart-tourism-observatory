# Long-Term Vision

## Purpose
Define the long-term ambition for SNTO.

## Background
The consensus review asked whether SNTO could become a global reference platform.

## Current State
The answer was yes, but only with focus, validation, operational maturity, and institutional trust.

## Evidence
Consensus probabilities from the 2026 baseline: international success 35%, commercial success 30%, scientific impact 60%, institutional adoption 40%.

## Recommendations
Long-term vision: become the reference decision layer for managing visitor-pressure risk and conservation investment in protected natural tourism destinations. This vision depends on validation, operational workflows, institutional trust, and integration with existing GIS and Earth observation ecosystems.

## Next Steps
Execute the three-year roadmap before claiming reference status.
