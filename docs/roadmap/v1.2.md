# v1.2 Roadmap

## Purpose
Define v1.2 as the controlled expansion and pilot-readiness release.

## Background
v1.2 should support controlled institutional pilots after v1.1 stabilizes positioning, evidence clarity, and validation design.

## Current State
SNTO is not yet ready for formal institutional reliance.

## Evidence
Protected-area and product reviews recommended paid pilots, field validation, GIS integration, and report outputs.

## Recommendations
v1.2 priorities:
- execute first field validation campaign;
- produce a procurement-ready paid pilot package;
- add initial GIS export or integration path;
- define asset-level action state;
- create evidence and report views for pilot stakeholders;
- avoid enterprise-scale tenancy, national benchmarking, or major UI rewrite.

## Next Steps
Secure pilot partners and measure decision-use outcomes, including whether SNTO improves inspection targeting, budget justification, and confidence in recommendations.
