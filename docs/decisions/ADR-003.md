# ADR-003: Require Validation Before Authoritative Scientific Claims

## Purpose
Record the scientific-claims decision.

## Background
The scientific review found SNTO promising but under-validated.

## Current State
SNTO can justify pilots and methods/software publication, but not authoritative ecological monitoring claims.

## Evidence
Scientific credibility was scored 68/100; empirical validation was the weakest dimension.

## Problem
Causal and operational claims may outrun the evidence.

## Context
Remote sensing can support decision-making but cannot alone prove visitor causality at protected-area asset scale.

## Options Considered
- Present SNTO as fully validated monitoring system.
- Present SNTO as exploratory dashboard.
- Present SNTO as a validation-aware decision-support framework.

## Decision
SNTO should claim decision-support and causal hypothesis generation until field validation and cross-territory replication are complete.

## Consequences
Positive: scientific credibility and reduced reviewer criticism. Negative: weaker marketing claims until validation improves.

Accepted from 2026 scientific and consensus reviews.
