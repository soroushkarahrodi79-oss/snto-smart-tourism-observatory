# ADR-008: Use Existing GIS and EO Platforms as Integration Surfaces

## Purpose
Record the GIS and Earth observation platform strategy.

## Background
The reviews concluded that SNTO should not compete directly with ArcGIS, Google Earth Engine, Sentinel Hub, or BI platforms.

## Current State
SNTO's differentiation is the decision layer above these systems.

## Evidence
The CPO and strategy reviews stated that SNTO loses as generic GIS or EO infrastructure, but can win as protected-area decision intelligence.

## Problem
Trying to replace established GIS/EO platforms would dilute SNTO and create unnecessary adoption barriers.

## Context
Protected-area agencies often already use GIS systems and expect exports, layers, and integrations.

## Options Considered
- Build a full GIS replacement.
- Build a full EO processing platform.
- Integrate with GIS/EO systems and own decision logic.

## Decision
SNTO should integrate with existing GIS and EO platforms, while owning evidence-to-decision workflows, confidence, prioritization, and reporting.

## Consequences
Positive: stronger adoption path and clearer differentiation. Negative: creates integration work and dependency on external systems.

## Status
Accepted from 2026 product and consensus reviews.
