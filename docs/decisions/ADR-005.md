# ADR-005: Require Authentication, Authorization, and Audit Before Enterprise Adoption

## Purpose
Record the identity, authorization, and audit decision.

## Background
The CTO review treated missing auth, authorization, tenancy, and audit as enterprise blockers.

## Current State
SNTO is suitable for demos or controlled pilots, not enterprise customers.

## Evidence
Security scored 25/100 and enterprise readiness 28/100.

## Problem
Institutional customers cannot rely on SNTO without controlled access, accountable actions, and auditable decision history.

## Context
Protected-area decisions involve public accountability, budgets, political reporting, and potentially restricted environmental data.

## Options Considered
- Keep product unauthenticated for simplicity.
- Add simple password protection.
- Require enterprise identity, role-based authorization, and audit events before enterprise adoption.

## Decision
Enterprise adoption requires authentication, role-based authorization, tenant-aware access, and audit logging.

## Consequences
Positive: enables institutional trust. Negative: adds platform complexity and delays enterprise readiness.

## Status
Accepted from 2026 CTO and consensus reviews.
