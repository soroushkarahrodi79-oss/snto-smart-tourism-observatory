# ADR-001: Treat SNTO as Protected-Area Decision Intelligence

## Purpose
Record the strategic product-category decision.

## Background
Multiple reviews warned that SNTO should not position itself as a generic GIS, dashboard, observatory, or remote-sensing platform.

## Current State
SNTO is currently broader than its strongest market position.

## Evidence
The CPO and consensus reviews identified "protected-area decision intelligence" as the strongest category.

## Problem
SNTO risks weak adoption if it presents itself as a broad observatory or dashboard.

## Context
GIS, Earth observation, and BI incumbents already dominate horizontal categories.

## Options Considered
- Generic GIS dashboard.
- Scientific observatory.
- Smart tourism platform.
- Protected-area decision intelligence.

## Decision
SNTO should be framed as protected-area decision intelligence for visitor-pressure risk and conservation investment.

## Consequences
Positive: clearer buyer, stronger differentiation, sharper roadmap. Negative: narrower market and need for focused validation.

## Status
Accepted from 2026 strategic consensus.
