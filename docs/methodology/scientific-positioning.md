# Scientific Positioning

## Purpose
Define SNTO's scientific identity and appropriate claims.

## Background
The reviews found SNTO strongest as a decision-support methodology linking remote sensing, ecological risk, confidence, and protected-area management.

## Current State
SNTO should be positioned as a scientific decision-support framework, not a fully validated ecological monitoring authority.

## Evidence
The scientific review scored SNTO 68/100 for credibility, with strong transparency and methodology but weaker empirical validation and causal proof.

## Recommendations
Use cautious language:
- "supports prioritization";
- "identifies visitor-pressure-consistent patterns";
- "generates causal hypotheses";
- "provides confidence-aware decision support."

Avoid unsupported claims:
- "proves visitor causation";
- "fully separates climate and tourism degradation";
- "authoritative monitoring system";
- "validated global model."

## Next Steps
Align publications, product materials, and methodology documents with this positioning.
