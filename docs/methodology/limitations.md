# Limitations

## Purpose
Provide the authoritative record of scientific limitations.

## Background
SNTO's transparency about limitations was one of its strongest scientific qualities.

## Current State
Key limitations:
- field validation is incomplete;
- causal attribution is vulnerable;
- indicators require external calibration;
- real, calibrated, synthetic, and simulated evidence must remain separated;
- generalizability beyond initial territories is unproven;
- uncertainty is recognized but not fully propagated;
- budget recommendations may appear more precise than evidence supports.

## Evidence
These limitations recur across scientific, director, product, and consensus reviews.

## Recommendations
Every scientific or institutional claim should reference the relevant limitation when evidence is incomplete.

## Next Steps
Turn limitations into validation and roadmap tasks.
