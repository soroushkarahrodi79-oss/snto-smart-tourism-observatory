# Product Vision

## Purpose
Define the product SNTO should become.

## Background
The reviews agreed that SNTO's strongest opportunity is not mapping or dashboards, but decision intelligence.

## Current State
SNTO currently behaves like an advanced observatory/demo. The desired product is an institutional decision system for protected natural tourism assets.

## Evidence
The consensus review stated that SNTO can become a reference only by narrowing to evidence-based decision intelligence for visitor pressure, ecological risk, and conservation investment.

## Recommendations
Product vision: help protected-area managers make defensible decisions about visitor-pressure risk and conservation investment.

## Next Steps
Use this vision to filter roadmap and UI decisions.
