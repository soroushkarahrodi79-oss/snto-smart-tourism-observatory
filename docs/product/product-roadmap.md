# Product Roadmap

## Purpose
Summarize product roadmap direction.

## Background
The roadmap must translate reviews into staged product maturity.

## Current State
v1.1 should focus on integration and stabilization. v1.2 should support controlled expansion and pilots. v2.0 should introduce modular product architecture and UI evolution. v3.0 should scale enterprise and institutional maturity.

## Evidence
The international consensus review gave a three-year roadmap: scientific/product narrowing, operationalization and validation, institutional scaling.

## Recommendations
Do not prioritize broad feature expansion before core decision workflow and validation. Design exploration should be isolated from v1.0 stability and staged mainly for v2.0.
