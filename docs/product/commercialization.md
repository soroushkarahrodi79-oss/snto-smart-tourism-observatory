# Commercialization

## Purpose
Document commercial viability and go-to-market assumptions.

## Background
The CPO review evaluated buyer, pricing potential, adoption risks, and investment potential.

## Current State
SNTO is commercially plausible but not yet proven. The best near-term path is paid pilot, not full enterprise sale.

## Evidence
The CPO review estimated commercial score 55/100 and suggested pilot pricing before enterprise packaging.

## Recommendations
Attach SNTO to existing public-sector budget lines: monitoring, visitor management, climate adaptation, conservation planning, sustainable tourism, or reporting.

## Next Steps
Build a procurement-ready pilot package with validation, reporting, and GIS integration scope.
