# Personas

## Purpose
Define buyer and user personas.

## Background
The CPO review separated economic buyer, daily users, and executive decision maker.

## Current State
Primary buyer: protected-area network director, national parks agency, regional environment/tourism ministry, conservation authority, or destination-management organization with protected natural assets.

Daily users:
- operations manager;
- conservation or GIS analyst;
- ranger/ecologist;
- policy/reporting officer.

Executive consumer:
- park director or senior public-sector leader.

## Evidence
The protected-area director review identified the director as a recurring decision maker, not the daily user.

## Recommendations
Design role-specific workflows and avoid forcing every user through the same dashboard.

## Next Steps
Define permissions, homepage, and report needs for each persona before v2.0.
