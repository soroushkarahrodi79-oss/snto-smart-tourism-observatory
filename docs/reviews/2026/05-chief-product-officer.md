# Chief Product Officer Review

## Purpose
Archive the product-only evaluation.

## Background
SNTO was evaluated for positioning, differentiation, buyer, workflows, pricing, commercialization, and product vision.

## Current State
SNTO is not a GIS, dashboard, or generic observatory. It is best positioned as decision intelligence for protected natural tourism destinations.

## Evidence
Scores:
- Product score: 68/100.
- Commercial score: 55/100.
- Market differentiation: 78/100.
- Long-term potential: high but conditional.

Core product recommendation: asset risk and intervention console.

## Recommendations
Build around the chain: ecological signal -> visitor-pressure inference -> confidence -> priority -> budget -> report.

## Next Steps
Package a paid pilot, define personas, and remove academic material from the primary product surface.
