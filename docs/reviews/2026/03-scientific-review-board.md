# Scientific Review Board

## Purpose
Archive the scientific validation assessment.

## Background
SNTO was reviewed by perspectives covering remote sensing, ecology, protected areas, environmental statistics, spatial analysis, and tourism research.

## Current State
SNTO is promising as a methodology and software framework, but not yet empirically validated enough for authoritative international operational use.

## Evidence
Scientific credibility: 68/100. Publication readiness is moderate for a methods/software paper and insufficient for a strong validation paper.

Most serious weaknesses:
- incomplete field validation;
- vulnerable causal claims;
- externally uncalibrated composite indicators;
- incomplete uncertainty propagation;
- unproven international generalizability.

## Recommendations
Use cautious claims and prioritize validation.

## Next Steps
Execute field validation, false-positive/false-negative analysis, and cross-territory replication.
