# International Review Board

## Purpose
Archive the multi-stakeholder international committee verdict.

## Background
The committee combined perspectives from park management, remote sensing, GIS, enterprise SaaS, product, government digital transformation, and conservation NGOs.

## Current State
SNTO can realistically become a global reference platform only if it narrows its mission and matures around validation, operations, enterprise trust, and institutional workflows.

## Evidence
Consensus probabilities:
- International success: 35%.
- Commercial success: 30%.
- Scientific impact: 60%.
- Institutional adoption: 40%.

## Recommendations
Stop presenting SNTO as an observatory. Rebuild the narrative around defensible decisions about visitor-pressure risk and conservation investment.

## Next Steps
Execute the three-year roadmap.
