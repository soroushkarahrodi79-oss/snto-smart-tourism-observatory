# Weaknesses

## Purpose
Archive SNTO's strategic weaknesses.

## Background
Weaknesses were identified across scientific, software, product, and institutional reviews.

## Current State
SNTO's weaknesses are serious but addressable through validation, focus, and platform hardening.

## Evidence
Top weaknesses:
1. Incomplete field validation.
2. Vulnerable causal claims.
3. Insufficient enterprise security.
4. No tenancy or audit model.
5. Product surface is too academic and broad.
6. Monolithic Streamlit architecture.
7. Incomplete operational workflow.
8. Limited commercial proof.
9. Unproven multi-territory generalizability.
10. No enterprise-grade adoption package.

## Recommendations
Treat weaknesses as roadmap gates, not optional improvements.

## Next Steps
Tie each major weakness to a release milestone and owner.
