# Opportunities

## Purpose
Archive the major opportunities identified by the reviews.

## Background
SNTO's opportunity is strongest where existing tools do not connect evidence to accountable management decisions.

## Current State
The largest opportunity is to become the decision layer between Earth observation/GIS data and protected-area management action.

## Evidence
Repeated opportunities:
- protected-area visitor-pressure risk;
- confidence-aware decision support;
- conservation budget prioritization;
- public accountability reporting;
- GIS/EO integration layer;
- validation-backed institutional pilots;
- multi-park benchmarking.

## Recommendations
Prioritize opportunities that strengthen the core decision chain.

## Next Steps
Use opportunities to shape v1.2 pilot proposals and v2.0 product architecture.
