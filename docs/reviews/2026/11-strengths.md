# Strengths

## Purpose
Archive SNTO's strategic strengths.

## Background
Strengths were identified across scientific, product, operational, and strategic reviews.

## Current State

Top strengths:
1. Evidence-to-decision chain.
2. Protected-area specificity.
3. Decision Confidence Score concept.
4. Visitor-pressure causal hypothesis.
5. Transparent limitations and provenance.
6. Budget prioritization logic.
7. Strong differentiation if narrowly positioned.
8. Potential institutional reporting value.
9. Good methods/software publication potential.
10. High innovation score.

## Recommendations
Protect these strengths from dilution by broad feature expansion.

## Next Steps
Use strengths in pilot narrative and scientific positioning.
