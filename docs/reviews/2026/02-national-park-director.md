# Protected-Area Director Review

## Purpose
Archive the protected-area buyer and executive-user perspective.

## Background
SNTO was evaluated as if being sold to a major protected natural area.

## Current State
A protected-area director would trust SNTO as an early-warning and strategic prioritization layer, not yet as an authoritative operational system.

## Evidence
Key conclusions:
- Overall score: 62/100.
- Purchase probability: 35% for full purchase, 65% for paid pilot.
- Useful decisions: inspection prioritization, staff allocation, budget narrative, visitor-pressure alerts, evidence gaps.
- Missing: field validation, official GIS integration, audit trail, task lifecycle, local procurement calibration.

## Recommendations
Make the home page a decision brief: priority assets, confidence, action, cost, owner, status, and political reporting summary.

## Next Steps
Build field verification, intervention tracking, and official reporting workflows before institutional reliance.
