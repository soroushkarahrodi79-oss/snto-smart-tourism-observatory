# Visualization Review

## Purpose
Document visualization strengths and weaknesses.

## Background
SNTO relies on maps, tables, charts, KPIs, and scientific visual explanations.

## Current State
Visualizations communicate breadth, but scientific decision views need stronger uncertainty, baseline, and evidence separation.

## Evidence
The scientific and product reviews recommended showing confidence and uncertainty where decisions are made, not only in methodological notes.

## Recommendations
Visualization priorities:
- distinguish real, calibrated, synthetic, and simulated evidence;
- show thresholds and confidence;
- avoid overprecision;
- connect chart states to decisions;
- provide exportable evidence summaries.

## Next Steps
Create visualization standards for EHS, DCS, SCM, TPI, and TIS.
