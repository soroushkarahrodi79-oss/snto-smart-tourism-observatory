# Future Strategy

## Purpose
Define the long-term strategic direction.

## Background
The consensus review concluded that SNTO can become a reference platform only through validation, focus, and operational maturity.

## Current State
SNTO has high innovation but moderate long-term sustainability unless it becomes a repeatable product.

## Evidence
Long-term sustainability was scored 58/100, while innovation was scored 82/100.

## Recommendations
Build the category carefully: protected-area decision intelligence for visitor-pressure and conservation investment.

## Next Steps
By year three, target multi-park deployment, institutional reporting, enterprise hardening, and measured outcomes.
