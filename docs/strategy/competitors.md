# Competitors

## Purpose
Define the competitive landscape.

## Background
The reviews compared SNTO with GIS, Earth observation, BI, and enterprise data platforms.

## Current State
SNTO should not compete directly with ArcGIS, Google Earth Engine, Sentinel Hub, Tableau, Power BI, or enterprise data platforms.

## Evidence
The product and CPO reviews concluded that SNTO's advantage is the decision layer above these tools.

## Recommendations
Frame competitors as infrastructure partners where possible. SNTO should own decision logic, confidence, workflows, and protected-area reporting.

## Next Steps
Prioritize integration and export strategies.
