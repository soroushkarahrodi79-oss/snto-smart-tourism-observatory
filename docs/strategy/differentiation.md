# Differentiation

## Purpose
Define SNTO's defensible differentiation.

## Background
The reviews found SNTO weak as a generic platform but strong as a vertical decision layer.

## Current State
Differentiation score: 78/100. The strongest differentiation is the evidence-to-decision chain.

## Evidence
Repeated differentiators:
- protected-area specificity;
- confidence-aware recommendations;
- visitor-pressure causal hypothesis;
- budget prioritization;
- public-sector accountability.

## Recommendations
Defend differentiation by staying narrow and integrating with broader platforms.

## Next Steps
Translate differentiation into pilot messaging and product workflows.
