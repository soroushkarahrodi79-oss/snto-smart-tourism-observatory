# Market Position

## Purpose
Define SNTO's market category and buyer context.

## Background
The product review found that broad positioning weakens SNTO.

## Current State
Recommended category: protected-area decision intelligence.

## Evidence
The consensus review stated that SNTO should be rebuilt around the institutional promise of defensible visitor-pressure and conservation-investment decisions.

## Recommendations
Lead with protected-area pain, not technology.

## Next Steps
Develop messaging for agencies, park networks, and conservation funders.
