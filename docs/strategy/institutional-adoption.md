# Institutional Adoption

## Purpose
Define what must be true for public institutions to adopt SNTO.

## Background
The protected-area director and government transformation perspectives emphasized accountability and auditability.

## Current State
SNTO is fit for controlled pilots, not formal institutional reliance.

## Evidence
Adoption blockers include missing field validation, audit trail, GIS integration, official reporting, role-based workflow, and procurement packaging.

## Recommendations
Build institutional credibility through pilots, reports, validation, and governance.

## Next Steps
Secure 2-3 protected-area pilots with explicit success criteria.
